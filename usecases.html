Use Cases

Orangewood builds affordable AI-powered robotic arms that are easy to operate. Backed by Y Combinator, Orangewood is headquartered in the US, with our R&D team in India.

Visual
Inspection

Equipped with high-resolution cameras and AI-powered image processing, Orangewood Lab’s robot can meticulously examine products for defects, inconsistencies, or abnormalities with unmatched accuracy.

From manufacturing lines to quality assurance processes, Orangewood Lab’s robot streamlines inspections, reducing human error and increasing productivity.

Bin
Picking

With its advanced capabilities, the robotic arm can seamlessly pick up objects of varying sizes, shapes, and weights, then precisely place them in predetermined locations with speed and accuracy.

Bartending
Bot

Orangewood Lab's presents a state-of-the-art AI and robot that expertly combines ingredients, shakes, stirs and pours with precision and flair.

Our bartending robot is programmed with an extensive repertoire of recipes, allowing it to create an endless array of perfectly mixed drinks. Whether it's a classic cocktail or a customized creation, Orangewood Labs' robot delivers consistent excellence, bringing a touch of automation and sophistication to the world of mixology.

Spray
Painting

In industries where spray painting poses risks to human workers, we offer a groundbreaking solution. Our advanced robot eliminates the dangers associated with spray painting, thereby paving the way for a safer and healthier work environment.

By harnessing the precision and efficiency of automation, the robot ensures flawless and consistent paint application while safeguarding the well-being of humans.

Powder
Coating

Orangewood combines robotics with state-of-the-art AI to deliver revolutionary powder coating technology to deliver exceptional results. Our robot can effortlessly apply precise and uniform layers of powdered coating material onto various surfaces, guaranteeing superior quality and durability.

The automated process ensures consistent application, reducing human error and increasing efficiency. Whether it's metal, plastic, or wood, Orangewood's robot is designed to handle a wide range of materials, providing a flawless finish that exceeds expectations. Experience the future of powder coating with Orangewood's robot.
