About Us

Orangewood Labs is a robotics company that is revolutionizing the industry by developing the world's most affordable robotic arm. With headquarters in SF, California, and an R&D team in Noida, India.




This objective recognizes that robotics technology has the potential to transform many areas of human activity, from manufacturing to healthcare to education, and that access to this technology should not be limited to a privileged few.

By democratizing robots, we can create a more equitable and inclusive future, where the benefits of robotics technology are available to all.

This can help to address some of the longstanding social and economic disparities that exist in our society and pave the way for a more sustainable and just future.
