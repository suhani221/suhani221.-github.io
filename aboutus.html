About Us

Orangewood builds affordable AI-powered robotic arms that are easy to operate. Backed by Y Combinator, Orangewood is headquartered in the US, with our R&D team in India.

We leverage the latest advancements in ML and generative AI to design and build highly efficient collaborative robots that are easy to operate.

Robotics can transform numerous areas of human activity, from manufacturing to healthcare to education.

The immense potential and applications of robotics motivate us to keep innovating to help as many people in as many ways as possible.

By democratizing robots, we can create a more equitable and inclusive future for the community.
